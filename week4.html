September, 20 2019

This week is the fourth week of fall semester 2019.


On Monday, We started another group project with the compsci senior design. I will be working together with compsci people as the operation team and will be responsible to set up the infrastructure for them. My team will be working on product b which is focusing on building a product that would allow professors to keep track of attendance seamlessly.


On Tuesday, my team started the planning on how our product is going to be like and started brainstorming ideas. We decided to have a two-factor verification by checking the radius and then scan the QR code for students to check-in.


On Wednesday, my team had a quick standup to see everybody's progress and then we started to do the sketching. Each of us sketched our ideas and after that we presented it to each other. Once we're done presenting our ideas, we all gave feedbacks to each other and ended it with voting and storyboarding.


On Thursday, we created our team agreement and finalized our tool selections.
